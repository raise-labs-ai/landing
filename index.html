About RAISE

RAISE (Responsible AI Scoring Engine) is an open initiative helping developers and companies build AI with confidence. From risk scoring to compliance checklists, our SDKs and APIs are made to integrate easily into your ML pipelines.

Key Features

  • Transparent AI risk scoring system
  • Open-source SDKs and APIs
  • Alignment with global AI regulations
  • Extensible rules engine with metadata
